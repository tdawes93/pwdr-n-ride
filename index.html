What are we about?

Pwdr 'N' Ride are a snowboarding collective based in Banff, Canada. We provide lessons, group ride events and host competitions across the Ski Big 3 resorts!


We welcome riders of all abilities from those never having set foot in snow to world class athletes.


Our aim is to build a community and share our love for the sport. Check out the photos and videos in our gallery!

  • Lessons:

    We provide both private and group snowboard lessons for all abilities and ages (min age 4 years old) at your resort of choice!

  • Group Events:

    We regularly host group ride events. In the past these events have ranged from park days to back country freeride events to girl shred days!

  • Competitions

    Our 4 annual competitions are always a must place event for any snowboarder! Whether you're just spectating or taking the plunge, you don't want to miss out!
